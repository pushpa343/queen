Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 6381568585, sending a WhatsApp message to +91 9486639475, or visiting our store directly. We recommend booking in advance for consultations.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Most garments are ready within 7-10 business days. For complex designs or embroidery work, it may take 2-3 weeks. We also offer express services for urgent requirements.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes, we provide home consultation and measurement services for premium customers. Please contact us to schedule a home visit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, bank transfers, and all major credit/debit cards. Advance payment is required for custom orders.

Do you provide alterations?

Yes, we offer alteration services for garments purchased from us. We also provide alterations for garments from other stores at competitive rates.
